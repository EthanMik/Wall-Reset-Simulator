Keyboard Controls:
                ────────────────────────────────────
                [W]       →  Move Up
                [A]       →  Move Left
                [S]       →  Move Right
                [D]       →  Move Down
                [←]       →  Turn Left
                [→]       →  Turn Right
                [1]       →  Toggle Front Laser Visibility
                [2]       →  Toggle Left Laser Visibility
                [3]       →  Toggle Right Laser Visibility
                [4]       →  Toggle Rear Laser Visibility
                [LMB]     →  Toggle Field View
